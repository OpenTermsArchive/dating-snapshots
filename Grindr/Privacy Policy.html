When you use the Services, we collect personal information that you choose to provide directly to us or that we collect automatically, such as data about your interactions with and use of the Services. We may also receive information about you from third parties.

Your profile is public, so you should not include information that you want to keep private.

●      Messages. We may also make inferences about you from the information we collect, such as inferences based on your activity and usage within the app.

 

 

Information we receive from other sources. Grindr collects some personal information from other sources, such as through payment processors (where we receive Purchase Information and Account Information), other users of our Services (where the information we receive depends on the nature of their request to us) and, authentication partners like Apple, Google and Meta (such as if you link your Instagram account to your Grindr profile) where we receive an authentication token.

When we describe "processing" of Personal Information, we mean how we collect, use, share, and retain Personal Information.

California Residents. Please see Your California Privacy Rights to learn about the specific rights you are afforded under California privacy laws.

PERSONAL INFORMATION WE COLLECT

We start to collect Personal Information from In-Scope Individuals during the recruitment or onboarding process and throughout and post the duration of your employment or your engagement with Grindr. We may collect Personal Information directly from you, directly from the devices you use to support the work you do for Grindr, or from third-parties. We have to collect some Personal Information from you in order to pay you, reimburse you for expenses, provide you with benefits, and fulfill legal obligations (e.g., tax reporting). The work product of Grindr employees, whether in paper record, computer files, recorded communications, or in any other storage format, belongs to Grindr, and that work product, and the tools used to generate that work product, are always subject to review and monitoring by Grindr.

In the course of conducting our business, we may monitor employees' activities, online and within the workplace. For example, we have the capability to monitor all employees' use of workplace hardware, software and tools (e.g., laptop, email, and chat).

This section is not meant to suggest that all employees will in fact be monitored or their actions subject to constant surveillance. We have no duty to monitor. This is meant to bring to your attention that such monitoring may occur and may result in the collection of Personal Information from employees (e.g., through their use of company resources) and may be subject to use in accordance with this Privacy Policy. When using Grindr-specific equipment or Grindr resources (e.g., employer-provided network), employees should not have any expectation of privacy with respect to their use of such equipment or resources.

제7조 (쿠키)

사용할 수 있습니다. 쿠키란 이용자가 해당 웹사이트를 방문한 이력을 컴퓨터에서 저장하여 회사에 알려주는 파일입니다.


이용자가 해당 웹사이트에 접속하면 회사는 접속 빈도, 방문 시간 등의 분석, 이용자 추적, 이벤트 참여 및 방문 빈도 등의 정보 파악
을 통한 업무상 통계 목적으로 쿠키를 사용할 수 있습니다.


이용자는 쿠키 설치에 대한 선택권을 가지고 있습니다. 이용자는 자신의 컴퓨터의 웹 브라우저에서 관련 기능을 조정하여 모든 쿠
