GRINDR PRIVACY AND COOKIE POLICY

Effective Date: 2021-07-23 or the date of user acceptance.

Welcome to Grindr’s Privacy and Cookie Policy. This Policy explains how Grindr LLC (“Grindr” or “we” or “us”) processes and puts you in control of your Personal Data. When we talk about processing Personal Data, we mean how we collect, use, share, and retain Personal Data. This policy covers these activities across all of our services (“Services” or “Grindr Services”).   We have included [LEARN MORE] page links where we describe additional details you may find helpful.

This policy covers the following Grindr Services: 

  • The websites  https://www.grindr.com and https://grindr.gay (“ Grindr Site ”),
  • The Grindr mobile applications (e.g., Grindr iOS, Grindr iOS Xtra, Grindr Android, Grindr Android Xtra, Grindr Lite, Grindr Unlimited) (“ Grindr App ”),
  • The website  https://grindrads.com (“ Grindr Ads ”),
  • The website  https://web.grindr.com (“ Grindr Web Chat, ” part of the Grindr App),
  • The website  https://grindrbloop.com (“ Bloop Site ”),
  • And features such as Live Stream, Video and Audio Chat, Circles, and Private Messaging.

Our Commitment to Privacy . As a global LGTBQ+ social networking application, the Grindr App allows you to share sensitive information about yourself, including your sexual orientation and precise location, with us, the service providers who assist in the running of our services, and other Grindr users. It's our goal to put you in control of as much of the personal data you share with the Grindr Services as possible. We recommend you read this Privacy and Cookie Policy, and our Terms and Conditions , carefully.  From time-to-time within the Grindr Services, you will see pop-up privacy notices meant to help you understand what you are participating in when you opt-in to certain features or services.    

Grindr LLC is a U.S.-based Company . By using the Grindr Services, you are transmitting information to the United States. If you are a resident of another country, note that the United States may not afford the same privacy protections as your country of residence. For Grindr Lite users or those communicating with Grindr Lite users: Grindr may utilize an Amazon Web Services relay server based in Mumbai, India. The relay server does not typically store user data but facilitates the transmission of user data to the United States.

When you use the Grindr Services, we collect Personal Data about you.  At Grindr, “Personal Data” means any information that allows someone to identify you directly. If you are aware that a child or minor has submitted Personal Data on the Grindr Services, please contact us using the information provided below, or using the in-app reporting tool, so that we can take appropriate action.

Revocation of your consent will only apply to future processing activities and will not apply retroactively.
